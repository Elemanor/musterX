# SEO Audit Checklist - Handyman Toronto Landing Page

## ✅ Compliance with Google's 2025 Best Practices

### 1. Above-the-fold (5-second intent satisfaction)
- ✅ **H1**: "Same-Day Handyman for Homes, Condos & Offices — Toronto/GTA"
- ✅ **NAP**: Business name (RenoNext Handyman) + phone (416-555-0123) + service hours + service area

- ✅ **Fast trust**: WSIB-cleared, Insured, Condo/Office COI, Licensed partners badges

- ✅ **Inline widget**: Three options (Book Now, Text Photos, Get Bids)

- ✅ **4-6 honest Q&As**: Condos/offices, electrical work, permits, drain issues

### 7. Reviews

### 8. Technical Excellence
- ✅ **Mobile-first**: Responsive design with Tailwind CSS
